Child and student concessions

Children aged 3 years and under travel free and do not require a ticket. All customers, including children aged 4 years and over, must travel with a valid ticket.

Children 4 years and over

Children aged 4-15 years and eligible secondary students 16 years and over travel on Child/Youth fares.

School students

Primary and secondary students can be entitled to free or subsidised school travel across NSW.

Tertiary and TAFE students

Eligible full-time tertiary and TAFE students are entitled to concession fares on public transport in NSW.

Apprentices and trainees

Eligible first, second and third year apprentices and trainees are entitled to certain concessions on public transport in NSW.
