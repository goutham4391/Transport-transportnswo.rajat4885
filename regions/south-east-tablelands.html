South East and Tablelands

Welcome to South East and Tablelands

Extending from the Southern Highlands in the north to the Snowy Mountains and Victorian border in the south and with the nation's capital at its heart, South East and Tablelands is one of the most geographically diverse regions in NSW. Major towns include Bega, Bowral, Goulburn, Yass, Queanbeyan, Batemans Bay, Moruya and the Port of Eden.
