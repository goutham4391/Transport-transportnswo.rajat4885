Regional coach and bus trials

NSW TrainLink and Transport for NSW are trialling new transport services to better connect regional communities.

Current trial services:

  • Broken Hill and Adelaide and Mildura
  • Conargo to Echuca-Moama
  • Forster to Coffs Harbour
  • Goulburn to Canberra
  • Tumut and Wagga Wagga
  • Wyangala and Canberra

Key benefits

These trials aim to provide new connections for regional communities for commuting, attending medical or business appointments, shopping, recreational activities and visiting family and friends.

Each trial follows extensive community engagement, which helps us understand the community need. Following the trial period, reviews are undertaken to determine if services should continue permanently.

Trial services are in addition to existing train and coach services.

Current trial services

Broken Hill to Adelaide and Mildura

Due to COVID-19, border restrictions may be in place. Find out more about current travel restrictions for South Australia and Victoria.

The trial coach services from Broken Hill to Adelaide and Mildura make it easy to attend medical appointments, go shopping, or to visit family and friends.

The Broken Hill to Adelaide service allows you to stay for one or more days in Adelaide with services departing Broken Hill on Monday and Friday, returning Tuesday and Sunday.

The Broken Hill to Mildura service allows you to stay for one or more days in Mildura with services departing and returning to Broken Hill on Wednesdays and Fridays. Services commence from Wednesday 20 January 2021.

  • Broken Hill to Adelaide and Mildura timetable (pdf 66KB)

Bookings are essential. Seasonal fares apply, plus you’ll save 10%* when you book online. Book your ticket.

*Conditions apply

Conargo to Echuca-Moama

Due to COVID-19, border restrictions may be in place. Find out more about current travel restrictions for Victoria.

You can get from Conargo to Echuca-Moama and back every Wednesday.

It’s easy and affordable. Simply book your seat, sit back, relax and let us do the driving.

  • Return services every Wednesday (except public holidays)
  • Space for luggage, shopping and prams
  • Air conditioning
  • Two wheelchair spaces and boarding assistance if needed.
  • Toilet facilities along the way if needed.
  • Connecting customers with NSW TrainLink services to Albury
