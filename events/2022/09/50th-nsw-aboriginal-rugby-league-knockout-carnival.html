Important travel advice

Footy fans are encouraged to plan their trip and consider all the available travel options ahead of time.

The closest station to both venues is Bomaderry on the South Coast Line however trackwork is taking place on the South Coast Line over the long weekend so allow extra travel time and plan your trip.

Free shuttle buses will run each day from Bomaderry Train Station to Bomaderry Sporting Complex and Rugby Park, Nowra. 

If you’re driving, travel with your group and use designated carparks. A free park and ride service will run from each carpark each day. Allow extra travel time as traffic will be heavier than usual, you can find real-time updates on traffic conditions at livetraffic.com.

Ticketing information

Opal

You can use an Opal card for all modes of public transport in Sydney and surrounding regions, including trains, buses, ferries and light rail.

Remember to top up your Opal card before you travel to avoid waiting in line at top-up machines. You can top up your card online or at an Opal retailer.

More ways to pay

If you have a contactless-enabled American Express, Mastercard or Visa debit or credit card, or a linked device, you can use it to pay for your travel by tapping on and tapping off at Opal card readers. Available on all public transport in the Opal network, contactless payments are another way to pay on the Opal network. Adult Opal fare pricing applies and you will receive Opal benefits, including daily and weekly fare caps.

Metro

Sydney Metro services are high-frequency, driverless trains running between Tallawong Station and Chatswood Station.

Metro services start from the early morning running every 10 minutes until late. Change at Epping to connect with services towards Bomaderry on the South Coast Line. Please note trackwork on the South Coast Line will affect your travel. 

For Metro service disruption information, check trackwork and travel alert or plan your trip using the Trip Planner.

Train

The closest station to both venues is Bomaderry on the South Coast Line, however, trackwork is taking place on the South Coast Line over the long weekend and buses are replacing trains between Bomaderry and Wollongong. You will need to change for a bus to complete your journey so allow extra travel time and plan your trip.

Free shuttle buses will run each day between 7am and 6:30pm from Bomaderry Train Station to Bomaderry Sporting Complex and Rugby Park, Nowra. 

  • On Friday, Saturday and Sunday, Route 5 will run to Bomaderry Sporting Complex and Rugby Park, Nowra every 45 minutes.
  • On Monday 3 October, Route 1M will run to Bomaderry Sporting Complex every 30 minutes.

Trains also run between Sydenham and the City Circle, stopping at St Peters and Erskineville.

Bus

Free return shuttle buses will run throughout the weekend between 7am and 6:30pm from various parking locations to both event venues:

To Bomaderry Sporting Complex

  • Route 1 (Bomaderry RSL, Nowra Anglican College) - every 30 minutes Friday, Saturday and Sunday 
  • Route 1M (Bomaderry Train Station, Nowra Anglican Coellege) - every 30 minutes on Monday only
  • Route 2 (Bernie Regan Sporting Complex, Bomaderry TAFE, Bomaderry Motor Inn) - every 45 minutes Friday, Saturday, Sunday and Monday

To Rugby Park, Nowra

  • Route 3 (Bridge Road Carpark, Archer Racecourse) - every 30 minutes on Friday, Saturday and Sunday
  • Route 4 (South Nowra Football Grounds, Shoalhaven Netball Courts, Lyrebird Sports Park) - every 45 minutes on Friday, Saturday and Sunday
  • Route 5 - every 45 minutes from Bomaderry Train Station to Bomaderry Sporting Complex and Rugby Park, Nowra on Friday, Saturday and Sunday

For more information on the shuttle services and bus stops, visit NSW Aboriginal Rugby League Knockout Carnival

For bus service disruption information, check travel alerts or plan your trip using the Trip Planner.

Light Rail

L1 Dulwich Hill Line

L1 Dulwich Hill line light rail services run every 15 minutes between Central Grand Concourse and Dulwich Hill from 6am to 11pm daily, and until midnight on Fridays. Allow extra time and plan ahead using the Trip Planner.

Change at Central for South Coast Line services to Bomaderry

Please note, due to planned weekend trackwork, buses are replacing trains between Wollongong and Bomaderry and some trains run to a changed timetable. You will need to change to a bus to complete your journey so allow extra travel time and plan your trip.

L2 Randwick and L3 Kingsford Lines

L2 Randwick light rail services run between Circular Quay and Randwick via Central Station (Chalmers Street) and L3 Kingsford line services run between Circular Quay and Juniors Kingsford via Central Station (Chalmers Street).

Change at Central for South Coast Line services to Bomaderry.

Please note, due to planned weekend trackwork, buses are replacing trains between Wollongong and Bomaderry and some trains run to a changed timetable. You will need to change to a bus to complete your journey so allow extra travel time and plan your trip.

For maps and service frequencies see Light rail service information. For Light rail service disruption information, check travel alerts or plan your trip using the Trip Planner.

Drive

Traffic is expected to be heavier than normal on major roads approaching Bomaderry including the Princes Highway, Moss Vale Road and Kangaroo Valley Road. Motorists should allow for extra travel time when driving through the area.

If you are planning on driving to the event, travel with your group and allow additional travel time.

For the latest traffic information check Live Traffic NSW or download a real-time traffic app.

Taxi

Nowra Radio Taxi. Call (02) 4421 0333 to book.

Parking

There is no parking at Bomaderry Sporting Complex or Rugby Park, Nowra. A park and ride service will be available throughout the weekend from various parking locations.

For Bomaderry Sporting Complex, parking options are available at Bomaderry RSL, Country Motor Group, Bernie Regan Sporting Complex, Nowra Anglican College and Bomaderry TAFE. 

For Rugby Park at Nowra, parking options are available at Archers Racecourse, South Nowra Football Grounds, Shoalhaven Netball Courts and Lyrebird Sports Park 

Visit NSW Aboriginal Rugby League Knockout Carnival for more information.

Accessibility

Accessible trips are shown in the Trip Planner with the wheelchair icon. You can select "accessible services only" in the Options to ensure you only see accessible services. Only trip plans with accessible train platforms, ferry wharves and buses will be presented. See accessible travel or download a transport app (select the "Accessibility" filter).
