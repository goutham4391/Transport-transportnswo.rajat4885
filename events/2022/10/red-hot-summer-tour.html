    Red Hot Summer Tour

Important travel advice

Attendees are encouraged to plan their trip and consider all the available travel options ahead of time.

The nearest stops to Bella Vista Farm are Bella Vista and Norwest on the North West Metro Line. Metro services start from the early morning running every 10 minutes until late.

Remember you'll need to use an Opal card or contactless payment card/device. Register your details online at Opal or via the app ahead of time.

If you’re driving, travel with your group and use nearby local parking. Allow extra travel time as traffic may be heavier than usual, you can find real-time updates on traffic conditions at livetraffic.com.

Ticketing information

Opal

You can use an Opal card for all modes of public transport in Sydney and surrounding regions, including metro, trains, buses, ferries and light rail.

Remember to top up your Opal card before you travel to avoid waiting in line at top-up machines. You can top up your card online or at an Opal retailer.

More ways to pay

If you have a contactless-enabled American Express, Mastercard or Visa debit or credit card, or a linked device, you can use it to pay for your travel by tapping on and tapping off at Opal card readers. Available on all public transport services including metro, trains, buses ferries and light rail, contactless payments are another way to pay on the Opal network. Adult Opal fare pricing applies and you will receive Opal benefits, including daily and weekly fare caps.

Metro

Sydney Metro services are high-frequency, driverless trains running between Tallawong Station and Chatswood Station.

Metro services start from the early morning running every 10 minutes until late. The nearest stops to Bella Vista Farm are Bella Vista and Norwest stations.

For Metro service disruption information, check trackwork and travel alerts or plan your trip using the Trip Planner.

Train

The closest station to Bella Vista Farm is Blacktown or Seven Hills where you can change for local bus services towards the venue. 

For train service disruptions check travel alerts or use the Trip Planner.

Trackwork

Weekend trackwork is taking place from 2am Saturday 15 October until 2am Monday 17 October and may affect how you travel to and from the event. Plan ahead using the Trip Planner:

  • City Circle: Trains run in one direction only, from Central first stop Town Hall. Some trains may start or end at Central, run to a changed timetable or leave from different platforms.
  • T1 Western Line: Penrith trains make extra stops at Flemington and Homebush. Richmond trains run between Richmond and Leppington only. From 10pm Saturday to 6am Sunday, Trains run to a changed timetable between Penrith or Richmond and Leppington, and between Strathfield and the City.
  • T2 Inner West & Leppington Line Buses replace the all-stations Homebush trains between Strathfield and Central. Trains run to a changed timetable between Leppington and the City via Granville, every 30 minutes and do not stop at Ashfield. T1 Western Line Penrith trains make extra stops at Flemington and Homebush. From 10pm Saturday until 6am Sunday, buses replace trains between Merrylands and Strathfield. Trains run to a changed timetable between Leppington and Penrith or Richmond, and between Strathfield and the City.
  • T3 Bankstown Line: Buses replace trains between Lidcombe and Bankstown. Trains run between Liverpool, Bankstown and the City, some to a changed timetable.
  • T8 Airport & South Line: Trains run to a changed timetable and stopping pattern. Revesby trains run between Revesby and Central only, via the Airport.
  • Blue Mountains Line: Trains may run to a changed timetable due to trackwork between Strathfield and Central.  From 10pm Saturday to 6am Sunday buses replace trains between Blacktown and Central. Trains run between Blue Mountains Line stations and Blacktown only.
  • Central Coast & Newcastle Line: Trains may run to a changed timetable due to trackwork between Strathfield and Central.
  • South Coast Line: Daily from 6am until 6pm, at Fairy Meadow and Towradgi, all trains towards Central leave from platform 2, not platform 1. Some trains from North Wollongong towards Central also leave from platform 2.

Trackwork is carried out on an ongoing basis to maintain the safety, reliability and efficiency of the Sydney Trains network. Trackwork may affect how you travel so allow extra travel time and plan ahead. For the latest information, check travel alerts and trackwork or use the Trip Planner.

Bus

Regular bus services stop within walking distance of Bella Vista Farm on Norwest Boulevarde and Elizabeth Macarthur Drive. Plan your trip using the Trip Planner.

For bus service disruptions check travel alerts or use the Trip Planner.

Light Rail

L1 Dulwich Hill Line

L1 Dulwich Hill line light rail services run every 15 minutes between Central Grand Concourse and Dulwich Hill from 6am to 11pm daily, and until midnight on Fridays. Plan your trip using the Trip Planner.

Change at Central to connect with T1 Western Line trains to Seven Hills or Blacktown to connect with a bus service to Bella Vista Farm.

L2 Randwick and L3 Kingsford Lines

L2 Randwick light rail services run between Circular Quay and Randwick via Central Station (Chalmers Street) and L3 Kingsford line services run between Circular Quay and Juniors Kingsford via Central Station (Chalmers Street). Services run regularly, plan your trip using Trip Planner.

Change at Central to connect with T1 Western Line trains to Seven Hills or Blacktown to connect with a bus service to Bella Vista Farm.

For maps and service frequencies see light rail service information. For light rail service disruption information, check travel alerts or plan your trip using the Trip Planner.

Drive

Traffic is expected to be busy on roads approaching Bella Vista Farm including Old Windsor Road, Norwest Boulevard, Windsor Road, Edgewater Road and Westwood Way. Allow additional travel time and plan to arrive early.

For the latest traffic information check Live Traffic NSW or download a real-time traffic app.

Taxi

For details of taxi networks servicing the Sydney area, visit Transport Operators.

Parking

Parking is available at the Bella Vista Farm in the outer grounds of the farm, just inside the entrance.

Accessibility

Accessible trips are shown in the Trip Planner with the wheelchair icon. You can select "accessible services only" in the Options to ensure you only see accessible services. Only trip plans with accessible train platforms, ferry wharves and buses will be presented. See accessible travel or download a transport app (select the "Accessibility" filter).
