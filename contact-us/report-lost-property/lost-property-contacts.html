Lost property contacts

If you have lost something on public transport in the Greater Sydney area or on a NSW TrainLink service, please submit lost property query. If the operator is not listed in this online form you will need to contact the operator of the service on which you travelled.

For lost property that you need urgently (such as essential medical equipment), phone 131 500.

Operator contacts

For information regarding Sydney Metro including contact details, please visit the Sydney Metro website.

Items left at NSW TrainLink station platforms or sales agents

If you leave something at a NSW TrainLink station or sales agent, it will be held there for seven days, and if it not claimed will be sent to the Lost Property Office.
  • Train station contact details
  • Sales agent contact details

After seven days contact the Lost Property Office (see above).
