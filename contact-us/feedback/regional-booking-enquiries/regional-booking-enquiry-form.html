Regional booking enquiry form

If you need help with booking NSW TrainLink Regional travel, you may find the information you need on help with booking. You can use the form below to ask us a question. As we may not be able to get back to you immediately, please call 13 22 32 (or +61 2 3814 0210 outside Australia) if you require urgent assistance.

You can also submit a suggestion, compliment or complaint about Regional travel.
