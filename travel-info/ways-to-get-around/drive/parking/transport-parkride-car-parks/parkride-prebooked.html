Park&Ride Prebooked commuter parking

Guarantee a parking spot when you catch public transport

We're trialling Park&Ride Prebooked at select car parks from February 2021, allowing you to reserve a parking spot by booking ahead of time before catching public transport.

Park&Ride Prebooked provides up to 18 hours parking for as little as $10*. All you have to do is use the Park&Ride Prebooked app to book your spot, and then complete a valid public transport trip.

The first Park&Ride Prebooked car park is now available at Ashfield.

You can download the Park&Ride Prebooked app from the App Store and Google Play.

How it works

How to get started and book your spot

  • Download the Park&Ride Prebooked application for free from the App Store and Google Play.
  • Open the Park&Ride Prebooked app.
  • Set up your account details including your license plate registration, which we'll use to recognise your vehicle at the entry.
  • Book your spot. Your booking is valid for up to 18 hours.
  • And you're ready to go!

Using Park&Ride Prebooked car parks

  • Prebook and pay for your spot using the Park&Ride Prebooked app.
  • Arrive at the Park&Ride car park within 30mins of your requested entry time and follow the signs to the dedicated Park&Ride Prebooked car park area.
    • At the Prebooked area entry gate, we’ll use license plate recognition to identify your car and the boom gate will open to let you enter.
    • Park in any available parking spot within the Prebooked parking area.
  • Take a valid public transport trip by tapping on and off using an accepted Opal card, or a credit/debit card or Opal card linked with a Transport Connect account.
  • When it's time to leave, drive to the exit and tap your Opal card (if not linked to a Transport Connect account) at the gate to verify your public transport trip. If you have linked your credit/debit card or Opal card with a Transport Connect account, there's no need to tap out when you leave the car park.
    • If you have taken a valid public transport trip, the boom gate will open and you can proceed to the exit of the car park.
    • Additional fees will be payable at the exit gate if you have not taken a valid public transport trip or you stay longer than 18 hours.

Note:

  • Opal single trip tickets, School Opal cards and contactless-enabled cards and devices (e.g. credit cards, debit cards, Apple Pay) are not accepted at Transport Park&Ride Prebooked car parks.
  • Where your most recent Opal card transaction has been for travel on the Manly Ferry, your trip will not be recognised at Transport Park&Ride Prebooked car parks and you will need to pay a parking fee.
  • Tap on reversals (where an Opal card is tapped on and tapped off at the same location within 30 minutes, and has not been used to complete a trip) cannot be used to validate free parking at Transport Park&Ride Prebooked car parks.
  • These conditions apply to all Transport Park&Ride Prebooked car parks at all times.
  • Car park users will be charged a parking fee where Transport Park&Ride Prebooked conditions are not met.
  • Some car parks use number plate recognition technology to record time of entry and exit.

 

Cost

Park&Ride Prebooked parking rates

When you book your spot, you will be charged:

  • $10 if spot is booked more than 24 hours in advance of time of booked entry
  • $15 if spot is booked less than 24 hours in advance of time of booked entry

The booking fees allows valid Opal customers to park for up to 18 hours from the time they enter the Prebooked car park.

If you have not used public transport or you have exceeded the 18 hour Prebooked parking window, you will be charged:

  • $30 if you have prebooked a spot but not taken a valid public transport trip and parked for 0-18 hours
  • $110 if you have not Prebooked a spot and parked for 0-18 hours
  • $50 every 24 hours, or part thereof, if you have parked for over 18 hours

You can pay any penalty fees with credit card or debit card at the Park&Ride Prebooked exit gate. You will be able to tap or insert your card into the device. Cash will not be accepted.

Note:

  • *Park&Ride Prebooked rates are subject to change
  • The booking fee allows valid Opal customers to park for up to 18 hours from the time they enter the Prebooked car park.
  • If you do not use public transport or if your parking exceeds 18 hours, additional fees and rates may apply at exit.
  • These fees and rates ensure that spots are made available for other Park&Ride Prebooked customers.

Privacy notice

Your Privacy

Transport for New South Wales (TfNSW) is committed to protecting our customers’ personal information in accordance with the Privacy and Personal Information Protection Act 1998 (NSW).

We collect certain information when drivers enter and exit Transport Park&Ride car parks so that we can determine if commuters are eligible for free parking. Upon entry to the car park, technology located within the access control system collects the vehicle’s number plate and records it with a time and date stamp. When exiting, the Opal card machine reads the Opal card number and matches this with the last valid tap off made with that Opal card on any public transport mode to check if a valid journey has occurred within 18 hours since the vehicle entered the car park.

We will only use the above information for this purpose or a directly related purpose. The Opal sequence number is retained only for as long as required to validate eligibility for free parking or to settle refunds. Hashed Opal card numbers and de-identified journey data may be stored by TfNSW and used for internal planning and research purposes.

Anonymised postcode information is also collected from Roads and Maritime Services for the purpose of assessing customer demand for future planning purposes.

Entry to a Transport Park&Ride commuter car park is conditional on the collection of this information.

Your information will be securely managed by, or on behalf of, TfNSW from its head office at 231 Elizabeth St, Sydney NSW 2000

You can ask to access or amend your personal information held by us at any time during business hours.

We will not disclose your personal information unless you consent, or we are permitted or required to by law.

For further information about how TfNSW manages your privacy, see the Opal Privacy Policy and TfNSW’s Privacy Management Plan, or contact us at privacy@transport.nsw gov.au.
