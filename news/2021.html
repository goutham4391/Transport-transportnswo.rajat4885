Transport News - 2021

    Employee Opal Cards are changing

    Opal Thursday 16 December 2021

    A new blue Employee Opal card was introduced in June 2021 and has replaced the grey Employee card. The replacement card program is almost finished and most employees have received their new blue Employee Opal card.

    Real-time trip updates and digital timetables for Armidale, Bathurst and Port Macquarie

    Bus Tuesday 07 December 2021

    Armidale, Bathurst and Port Macquarie bus customers can now plan bus trips and make better-informed travel decisions using digital timetable information and real-time updates.

    North Sydney wharf upgrades

    Ferry Monday 08 November 2021

    North Sydney wharf will be temporarily closed from Monday 22 November for around six months.

    North West and North Shore Sydney bus service changes

    Bus Thursday 07 October 2021

    From Monday18 October there will be bus service changes across Western, North Shore and North West Sydney bus routes.

    Watsons Bay Wharf service changes

    Ferry Monday 30 August 2021

    From Monday 6 September, Sydney Ferries will not use Watson Bay Wharf while maintenance work takes place.

    Better bus services for Bathurst

    Bus Monday 26 July 2021
