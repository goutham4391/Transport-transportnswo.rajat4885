New bus route names and numbers in Orange and Griffith

Monday 29 August 2022

From Monday 12 September, bus route names and numbers are changing in Orange and Griffith to improve the consistency and accuracy of bus service information across NSW.

There will be no changes to timetables or route paths.

Look up your current school or regular bus route number below to see the new route number and name.

For school bus changes, there will be a transition period to help students and teachers during which each bus will display its old route names/number next to the new one.

Please note, from 12 September you will be able to plan your new trip using the Trip Planner.

If you have any further questions about these changes, please contact your local bus operator directly.
