Temporary bus diversions for Marine Drive, Wollongong

Monday 23 May 2022

Marine Drive, Wollongong will be closed to traffic from Monday 23 May 2022, for maintenance works in preparation of the UCI World Cycling Championships.

The traffic changes will result in route diversions for Premier Charters, Premier Motor Services, Dions and  Busabout route 887 and the closure of two bus stops:

  • Crown Street, opp WIN Entertainment Centre (250081)
  • WIN Entertainment Centre, Crown Street (250028)

This service change will be in place for approximately four months leading up to and including the event period (18-25 September 2022).

Marine Drive will reopen for buses and revert to their original route path after this date.

Check travel alerts, see onsite signage and plan your trip for up-to-date real time information.
